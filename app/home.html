Welcome to our Countries & Capitals application!



The application allows you to search and visualize data about countries and capital cities. To browse the data, please click the BROWSE COUNTRIES button.



Please feel free to browse each country's brief information. If you would like to see the details of each country, please click on the column where the country is shown on.



If you want to go back to home page or data list, please feel free to click the buttons in the buttom.
